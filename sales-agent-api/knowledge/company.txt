DinamikPOS — Restaurant Point of Sale (Company Knowledge Base)

ABOUT THE COMPANY
DinamikPOS is a B2B software company focused on restaurant and café operations. We sell cloud-connected POS terminals, kitchen display systems (KDS), handheld order tablets, and back-office reporting. Headquarters support is available on business days; 24/7 phone support is included on Enterprise plans only.

PRODUCT LINES
1) DinamikPOS Lite — Single terminal, one location, basic menu and cash/card payments. Best for small cafés and kiosks. Includes end-of-day reports and basic inventory counts.
2) DinamikPOS Pro — Multi-terminal, table layout, split bills, staff roles, and integrations with common accounting exports. Recommended for full-service restaurants with one or more branches.
3) DinamikPOS Enterprise — Central menu and pricing for many locations, API access, advanced analytics, loyalty integrations, and dedicated account manager. Minimum contract 12 months.

HARDWARE
We offer certified Android tablets and fixed POS terminals with receipt printers. Printers are thermal 80mm; spare rolls are sold separately. Hardware warranty is 24 months when purchased through DinamikPOS; third-party devices are not supported for warranty claims.

KITCHEN DISPLAY (KDS)
KDS is available on Pro and Enterprise. Orders route to kitchen stations by category (grill, cold, bar). Bump-to-complete reduces ticket errors. Lite does not include KDS.

PAYMENTS
We support major bank POS integrations in Turkey (bank-specific; list updated quarterly). Online payment links for delivery orders are available on Pro and Enterprise. DinamikPOS does not store full card numbers; processing follows PCI-DSS aligned practices via certified partners.

PRICING (INDICATIVE — CONFIRM WITH SALES)
Lite: monthly software fee per terminal (contact sales for current campaign pricing).
Pro: per terminal + optional KDS screen license.
Enterprise: per location bundle; API and analytics modules priced separately.
Setup and training fees may apply for multi-site rollouts.

IMPLEMENTATION
Typical go-live: 2–4 weeks for single site after menu import; multi-branch may take longer. We provide remote onboarding and on-site training where contracted.

DATA AND COMPLIANCE
Customer data is hosted in EU-region cloud options where available. Backups are daily; point-in-time restore is on Enterprise. Lite retains 90 days of detailed transaction logs in portal; longer retention on higher tiers.
